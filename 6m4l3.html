Lesson 4.3: Sustainability & Environmental Policy

This lesson explores how sustainability is achieved through environmental policy and various tools designed to protect the Earth while supporting development.

Step 1: What Is Sustainability?

Definition: Using resources in a way that meets current needs without compromising future generationsâ€™ ability to meet their needs.

Simple Explanation: Like not spending all your money in one day so that you have enough for tomorrow.

Step 2: Environmental Policy

Definition: Rules and laws that protect the environment by regulating resource use and pollution.

Analogy: Think of these policies as the rules of a game that keep the playing field fair and safe.

Step 3: Tools for Achieving Sustainability

  • Impact Assessments: Studies that predict the environmental effects of a project.
  • Renewable Energy: Transitioning to solar, wind, etc., to reduce fossil fuel use.
  • Conservation Programs: Projects to protect and restore natural ecosystems.

Analogy: Like balancing a household budget to ensure you have enough resources for today and tomorrow.

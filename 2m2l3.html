Lesson 2.3: Optimization Techniques

Optimization refines algorithms to improve performance. Learn strategies to reduce time and memory usage.

Why Optimize?

Improved performance is crucial for real-world applications, especially with large datasets.

Common Optimization Strategies

  • Choosing the Right Data Structure: e.g., using a hash table instead of an array.
  • Divide and Conquer: Break a problem into smaller parts (e.g., merge sort runs in O(n log n) time).
  • Memoization & Dynamic Programming: Cache results to avoid redundant calculations.
  • Eliminating Redundancy: Remove unnecessary loops or computations.
